Welcome to GradeLift

Welcome to GradeLift, a platform dedicated to providing free and accessible tutoring for students in grades K-12. Founded by Tanvir Chahal, GradeLift aims to help students excel in their studies while fostering a spirit of community service among high school volunteers.

Contact: Tanvir Chahal - tanvirc2418@gmail.com

"The reason I want to do this is so I can provide free and easily accessible tutoring to kids in grades K–12." - Tanvir Chahal

Apply to Become a Tutor

To apply to become a tutor, please ensure you meet the following criteria and email your application to tanvirc2418@gmail.com:

  • Full Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Reason why you want to become a tutor
  • A picture of some form of ID (e.g., Driver's License, School ID)
